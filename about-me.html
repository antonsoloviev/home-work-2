О себе

t was a warm summer day, about 72 degrees inside and hotter outside, I was watching some videos on YouTube when one of my friends sent me a video with a welcoming first frame of a man and another man standing next to each other. After sending me the video, my friend wrote, "nigga play the vid on full blast its gon be funni af" So I obliged and turned up my volume all the way until it reached 100. Right when I played the video it cut to two men, one black, having oral sex with each other. I was shocked at that very moment, and if it couldn't get any worse my parents walked into the room at the exact same time, I turned to them while the video continued playing behind my confused and distraught face. I was frozen, I couldn't make a sound or say a single word. Two days later my parents called me into their room and closed the door, causing all the light to leave the room, while they packed a suitcase they began to tell me that I would be leaving the house until they felt I could come back, little did I know, this would be the last time I see my parents. Now, as a 43 year-old living in Oakland, I regret every moment of this sad and unavoidable event.
